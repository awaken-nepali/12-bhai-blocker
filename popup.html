12 Bhai Media Blocker

This extension helps users avoid misleading or propagandistic content from the 12 Bhai Media network. By activating this extension, you will automatically block access to websites associated with 12 Bhai Media, protecting yourself from potential misinformation and biased content.

Note: This extension is designed to enhance your browsing experience by filtering content that may be harmful or unreliable.
